Hyperion.NG for webOS

by satgit62

Bring your Ambilight experience to the next level. Control your Hyperion.NG setup directly from your LG webOS TV – no additional devices required.

✨ Features

  • The webOS Loader has been optimised for use with Hyperion-webOS (PicCap), and it runs even when the NV12 option is enabled in PicCap
  • Native RGBW support
  • A wide range of LED controllers are supported
  • Direct connection to your HyperHDR server
  • Real-time Ambilight control
  • Adjust brightness, colors and effects
  • Fast and lightweight
  • Optimized for TV usage

✅ Requirements

  • Running Hyperion.NG instance in your network
  • LG webOS TV with Homebrew Channel
  • Root access may be required
